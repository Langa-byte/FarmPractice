Welcome to KwaNtshangase Dairy

Orange Grove Dairy was established in 1927 and is nestled in the foothills of the Biggersberg Mountains on Glynton Farm. This family business has expanded beyond its town boundaries to deliver great quality products across multiple provinces.

Our ultra-high-tech and newly rebuilt factories produce Milk, Maas, Yoghurt, Cream, Juices, asemZansi AMAHEWU and multiGo, an ancient grain and yoghurt blend.

Orange Grove is a Proudly South African company that seeks to source locally and provide products which are antibiotic free.

Live the Difference when You enjoy Orange Grove Products and Join us in being:

  • Water Wise: Water in our Factories is recycled – we don’t want to waste a drop!
  • Eco Friendly: Our packaging is plant-based for increased sustainability, renewability and recycling.
  • Community Caring: We invest in community outreaches, education and sports events, dance troops and safe havens…From National Crime boards to Local Feeding Schemes: We Care and Believe in Our Shared South African Future.
